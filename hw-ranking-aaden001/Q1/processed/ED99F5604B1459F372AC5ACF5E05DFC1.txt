The coronavirus pandemic has changed not only how we live but where we live, and that has upended some of the usual trends in local housing markets. Now that some people can work from anywhere, they can also live anywhere, and they are moving to different markets for all kinds of reasons, from affordability to climate to ease of everyday life.

ASTM develops standard for consumer masks
2/24/2021
After seven months of testing, ASTM International has created a national mask standard that designates minimum fit, performance, design and testing requirements for consumer face masks. Products adhering to the guidelines would be labeled with level one certification, which requires the filtration of at least 20% of particles, or level two certification, which filters at least 50% of particles but has lower breathability.

Looking for easy ways to maximize your wifi speeds? We're here to help with our best list of wifi tips and tricks. Plus, get the most from your home wifi network by opening your Panoramic Wifi app.

Wifi pro tips






	
Pick the right placement

Put your router in a central location in your home to get the strongest connection possible.







	
Out with the old

Older devices can slow down your network. Switch them off when they're not in use to keep your network running smoothly.







	
Stay up to speed

Disconnect any unused devices from your network while you're not using them to speed things up.
